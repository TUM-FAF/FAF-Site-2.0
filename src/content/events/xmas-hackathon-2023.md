## XMas FAF Hackathon 2023: A Technological Adventure on the Eve of the Holidays

With enthusiasm and a touch of nostalgia, the XMas FAF Hackathon has come to an end, leaving behind unforgettable memories and promising projects. The event, organized by FAF NGO, proved to be a captivating experience for everyone involved.

From December 23–24, the Tekwill innovation center became the epicenter of creativity and innovative technological solutions. The **75 participants**, eager to put their knowledge to the test, worked intensely for 24 hours to solve the proposed challenges: timetable generation and handwriting recognition using OCR technology. With every line of code and every new idea, a vibrant environment of collaboration and innovation took shape.

Among the solutions presented by the **15 teams**, several impressive projects stood out, demonstrating not only the students' technical skills but also their creativity and teamwork.

---

## Award-Winning Projects

- **Schedule Generator** — A specially designed algorithm for the efficient generation of university timetables, accompanied by a user-friendly website for administration. Through this site, administrators can add or modify information in the database, while students can access it with ease.

- **ScheduleExpert** — A web-based timetable generation system with an emphasis on automation and flexibility. A key feature implemented is the system's ability to manage teacher substitutions, providing error precision that ensures educational continuity despite unforeseen changes.

- **OCR Model** — A system capable of recognizing simple handwriting and handwritten tables in English, transforming them into digital format. This model contributes to streamlining the process of converting manually written information into an easily manageable and accessible format.
