## XMAS FAF Hackathon 2024

A new edition of XMAS FAF Hackathon 2024 has successfully concluded, leaving behind unforgettable moments and innovative solutions that will linger in the participants' memories for a long time.

On December 21–22, Tekwill became the hub where **90 students** from the Technical University of Moldova spent 24 hours of tireless work in a festive environment filled with energy, competition, and collaboration.

Participants faced complex technological challenges that tested advanced skills in programming, software development, and innovation. The projects presented reflected not only accumulated knowledge but also the creativity and team spirit of the students.

---

## Highlighted Projects

Among the **19 projects** presented, the most remarkable were:

- **Fairy Desk** — A website for file management, evaluation, and completion assistance.
- **Clausify** — A web application that helps Santa Claus manage, visualize, and interact with children's wishes while controlling his environment of elves and Rudolph.
- **Renovo** — An application that assists Santa in solving problems and helps him deliver gifts more efficiently.
- **2much2ki** — An interactive desktop application that keeps you company, gets upset if ignored, and challenges you to play with it to make it happy by collecting gifts together.
