## Programmers' Day 2024

Every year, September brings us the opportunity to celebrate the technological genius that shapes our digital worlds. In 2024, FAF NGO brought together tech enthusiasts and IT professionals to mark Programmer's Day in a truly special way.

The event was a perfect blend of knowledge, innovation, and networking. Throughout the evening, we had the pleasure of listening to presentations from industry experts who offered fresh perspectives and inspiration:

- **Vladimir Stojoc** — Senior Machine Learning Architect at Fuselab Creative
- **Mihai Moglan** — Founder & CEO at SengEnergy, Machine Learning Engineer
- **Egor Șebarșov** — RPA Development Team Leader
- **Serkan Kazar** — Director of IDS Department
- **Marinela Lupusor** — Software Developer at Amdaris
- **Cristina Matciac** — Cegeka Site Manager, Moldova Office
- **Dorian Vicol** — Solutions Architect at Cegeka
- **Tudor Sclifos** — Data Analyst at EFES Moldova

Following the conference, thanks to the generosity of Orange and Amdaris, participants competed in a challenging quiz with prizes for the most creative and informed minds. The evening concluded with an excellent after-party, ideal for networking and building new connections.
