## Who do you think won the Summer FAF x Sigmoid Hackathon?

For two days at Cybercor, **24 teams** — totaling **120 students** — competed to build the best MVP. There were **3 topics** and **3 prizes of €1,000 each**.

For each challenge, 8 teams fought for the grand prize. Here are the winners for each category:
