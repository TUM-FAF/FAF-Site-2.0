## Who do you think won the XMAS FAF Hackathon 2025?

For two days at Tekwill, the FAF community gathered for a holiday-vibe hackathon where teams of tech-passionate students built real MVPs, collaborated intensely, and brought their ideas to life.

With **3 challenges** and **3 winning teams**, total prizes amounted to **€2,000 + gadgets**. For each challenge, multiple teams competed to deliver the most robust technical solution. Here are the winners for each category:
