## Programmers' Day 2023

Every year, September brings a unique opportunity to celebrate the genius behind the lines of code and complex algorithms that transform our lives.

In 2023, the tradition of spending Programmer's Day with FAF NGO continued to shine, gathering tech enthusiasts, developers, and IT professionals under one roof. This year's event was a captivating blend of knowledge, innovation, and authentic connections, creating a vibrant and high-energy atmosphere.

---

## Conference Highlights

During the conference, the stage was lit by the presence of enthusiastic specialists eager to share their valuable experiences:

- **Maxim Chiriac** (Assistant Professor, UTM) — *"The Importance of Hardware Programming in Present and Future Technologies"*
- **Elena Putina** (Lead UX/UI Designer, Enspire Creative) — *"Only Designers do UX… and Other Fairy Tales"*
- **Vasile Rotaru** (QA Engineer, ICG Engineering) — *"AI vs. the Educational System"*
- **Marina Bzovîi** (Executive Director, ATIC) — *"Law in the IT Field"*
- **Elena Danilova** (CRO Developer, Orange Systems) — *"A/B Testing: An Efficient Method for Testing and Optimizing User Experience"*
- **Traian Chivriga** (CEO, XY Partners) — *"How to Startup?"*
- **Dragoș Străinu** (Software Engineer, Planable) — *"Decisions and Choosing a Job in IT"*
- **Dumitru Vlah** (Project Manager, Tekwill Academy) — *"The IT Market – Competition, Trends, Training Programs, Tips & Tricks..."*

Their presentations were a source of inspiration, highlighting the diversity and complexity of the IT industry and leaving a significant mark on the professional journeys of everyone in attendance.

---

## Competition and Networking

A major highlight was the **technical quiz**, which tested participants' skills in a fun and challenging way. With a spirit of friendly competition, attendees fought for rewards based on their technical knowledge.

The subsequent networking session provided a precious chance for participants to interact and exchange ideas with colleagues, potential collaborators, and employers scouting for new talent.
