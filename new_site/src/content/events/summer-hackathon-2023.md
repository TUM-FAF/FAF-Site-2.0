## Summer FAF Hackathon 2023

The Summer FAF Hackathon 2023, held from June 16–17, put the creativity and passion for technology of **13 teams from UTM** to the test.

The primary objective of the Hackathon was to address the various challenges students face in universities, giving participants the opportunity to explore and tackle critical issues affecting their lives.

Students engaged in a broad spectrum of software projects, ranging from social media platforms to artificial intelligence solutions. The enthusiasm shown by the participants was maintained until the early morning hours, demonstrating a competitive spirit and a keen interest in continuous development.

At the climax of the event, after **24 intense hours**, participants presented their projects, leaving an impressive mark. The event sponsors awarded the best projects, further motivating the students on their path toward innovation.

Throughout the Hackathon, participants were guided by a team of mentors who were available to answer questions, provide technical assistance, and offer valuable insights, helping the teams excel.
