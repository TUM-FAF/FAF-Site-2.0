How It Works

From application to your first event — here's the path to becoming a FAF volunteer.

1

Apply

Fill out a short form telling us about yourself, your skills, and which department interests you.

2

Meet the Team

We'll invite you for a casual chat to get to know you and find the best fit for your interests.

3

Join a Team

Get placed under one of our leads — CRM, HR, Design, or PR — based on your skills and interests.

4

Make an Impact

Help organize hackathons, create content, design visuals, or coordinate with partners. You're in.

Our Team Structure

FAF is led by a focused team of student leaders. Here's how we're organized — and where you could fit in.

President ›

Sets the overall vision and direction for FAF. Coordinates across all departments, represents the organization externally, and ensures every project aligns with our mission of connecting Moldova's IT community.

Vice President ›

Supports the President in day‑to‑day operations, steps in when needed, and oversees cross‑department collaboration. The VP ensures deadlines are met and the team stays on track during event season.

CRM Lead ›

Manages relationships with partners, sponsors, and external stakeholders. Handles outreach, negotiates sponsorship packages, and maintains the network of companies that support FAF events.

HR Lead ›

Recruits and onboards new volunteers, organizes internal team‑building activities, and keeps the FAF community healthy and motivated. HR is the heartbeat of our volunteer culture.

Design Lead ›

Owns the visual identity of FAF — from event banners and social media graphics to merchandise and presentation decks. Leads a small design team to keep everything on‑brand and polished.

PR Lead ›

Runs FAF's public‑facing communications — social media, press coverage, community announcements, and content strategy. Grows our reach and makes sure every event gets the attention it deserves.

Why Volunteer with FAF?

It's not just about helping out — it's about growing together.

Real Experience

Organize events with 150+ participants, manage budgets, and work with real companies. This goes straight on your CV.

Network

Meet industry professionals, mentors, and fellow students who share your passion for technology.

Community

Movie nights, team outings, and a tight‑knit group of people who actually care about what they're building.

Growth

Develop soft skills — leadership, communication, project management — in a supportive environment where mistakes are learning opportunities.
